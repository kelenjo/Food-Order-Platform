About Kelenjo

Bringing flavors to life since 2010

Our Story

Kelenjo started as a small family restaurant in Tbilisi, with a simple idea: serve fresh and authentic meals made with love. Over the years, we’ve grown into a community favorite, known for blending tradition with modern taste.

Our Growth

From our humble beginnings, we’ve expanded our menu and services, embracing innovation while staying true to our roots. Today, Kelenjo offers dine-in, delivery, and catering for special events.
